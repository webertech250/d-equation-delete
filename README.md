# D-Equation 20 – Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

1. Open the app **Settings → Support**.
2. Send an email to **ikodesharwanda@gmail.com** with the subject **"Delete my account"**. Include the email you used to sign up.

**Data that will be deleted:**
- Account information (name, email, phone number)
- Listings you created in the app
- Other personal content

**Data that may be retained:**
- Anonymous analytics for app performance
- Records required by law for a limited period

**Processing time:** Requests will be completed within **30 days**.
